## Figma

<p>
  Figma is a collaborative design tool that allows teams to create, prototype, and share
  designs in real-time. It's known for its ease of use, powerful features, and ability to
  streamline the design process from start to finish
</p>
<a href="https://www.figma.com/"> Figma </a>

## Stark

 <img src="./stark-screenshot.png" alt="picture of Stark screens" width="auto" height="auto"/>
 <p>

Stark is a suite of integrated tools designed to help teams create accessible products. It offers features like contrast checking, vision simulation, and alt-text annotations, making it easier to identify and fix accessibility issues in designs

 </p>
 <a href="https://www.getstark.co/"> Stark </a>
